Few Words About Us

We provide 100% healthy, clean and hygenic food of all indian vegeterian categories.

We provide breakfast such as roti (flatbread), dosas (thin crepes made of lentils) or idlis (steamed rice-dough pancakes), and different dips and chutneys, as well as spiced potatoes.

Lunch

We provide different thalis in lunch such as gujarati thali, rajasthani thali and punjabi thali.

Dinner

In dinner, additional to thalis we provide different dishes such as bhajipav , pizzas, etc.
